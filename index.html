information about Kairat Nurtas

Kairat Nurtas or Qairat Nurtas (Kazakh: Қайрат Нұртасұлы Айдарбеков, Qaırat Nurtasuly Aıdarbekov; born 25 February 1989, Turkestan) is a Kazakhstan singer. The highest paid singer in Kazakhstan. the record of earnings from one toy is € 6100.20 He has topped the rating of the most popular local Kazakh musicians, based on CD sales He made his debut on the stage when he was 10 years old in Baikonur. He came forward together with such performers, as Meirambek Bespaev, Nurlan Onerbayev and Toktar Serikov. Kairat released his first single and his debut album in 2006 but his breakthrough came when he gave his first solo concert in 2008 in Almaty. Although his level of recognition was low, after that concert, his popularity started a continuous rise. He has interpreted the songs of prominent Kazakh composers such as Shamshy Kaldayakov, Aset Beiseuov, Erzhan Serikbayev. The premiere of feature film, Ökiniş, (Regret) was announced in April 2013, being a melodramatic history about his life as a singer. His mother Gulzira Aidarbekova produces and sponsors his performances.
It is known that Kairat Nurtas is married. The second half of the artist's name is Zhuldyz Abdukarimova. The girl also graduated from KazNAI named after T. Zhurgenov, although she is from Astana. The girl is a year older than her husband. The wedding took place in 2007. His wife has already given Kairat four children - daughters Sezim and Alau, sons Narul and Khan.
Motherhood does not prevent Zhuldyz from realizing her ambitions in the acting field, she regularly appears on the screen and has already fallen in love with the audience. And in 2018 for the main role in the film "Arman. When angels sleep" Zhuldyz was awarded the award from the Association of Film Critics in the category "Best Actress".

biography


The first solo concert of Kairat Nurtas took place on the day of his coming of age in Almaty in 2008. It was held in the Palace of the Republic. It is noteworthy that an hour before the performance, the hall was only half full. But a few minutes before the start, there was not a single empty seat in it – all tickets were sold out. So the stellar biography of Kairat Nurtas started from the first full house. Since then, the mega-popular singer in Kazakhstan has started a tradition – to give a big solo concert on every regular birthday.
With equal success, Kairat Nurtas performs songs by young little-known composers and hits of Kazakh pop classics - Asset Beiseuov, Shamshi Kaldayakov and others. For a long time, Kairat sang in a duet with the famous pop singer Syrym Isabayev, together with whom he performed the song "Merging of Hearts", which became a hit.

The career of Kairat Nurtas that began turned out to be extremely rapid. Now the artist's repertoire includes more than a hundred songs and dozens of CDs. The absolute advantage of the Kazakh singer is the live performance of author's songs at concerts. It is noteworthy that Kairat Nurtas is loved by young fans of talent and the older generation. The hits "Kaida Kaida" and "Baralmadym" were included in the list of the best songs of Kazakhstan.

personal life


The fit singer with a charming smile, whose height is 171 cm and weight does not exceed 65 kg, has a large army of fans who have always been interested in the personal life of the idol. But Kairat Nurtas was extremely reluctant to talk about this topic, arousing even greater interest in his own person.
Kairat Nurtas' hobby is horse riding. For the sake of passion, the singer acquired several thoroughbred horses. Often jockey photos appear on the artist's Instagram. And he also assembled an impressive fleet of vehicles, where, in addition to sports cars, there are also rare models on which Nurtas likes to ride around the city. The musician does not forget about his relatives. Gulzira Aidarbekova's mother, who is his producer, was presented with a Lexus RX crossover by the singer. The license plate of the SUV turned out to be difficult - 777gul.

Interestingly, Nurtas hid his family from the public for 10 years, even with his wife Kairat tried not to appear in public places, but in 2018 he presented joint photos of his wife and children to the delight of fans. Kairat is scrupulous about the appearance of his wife, not even allowing her to appear on the beach in open swimsuits, and in public - in transparent outfits. At all events, Zhuldyz is present in elegant floor-length dresses.
